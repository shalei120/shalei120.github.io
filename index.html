Resume
I'm always looking for highly motivated students to work with me on broad NLP research. Please feel free to reach out and apply!

I am now a professor in the Artificial Intelligence Institute of Beihang University.

Previously, I was a research associate in University of Oxford under the supervision of Prof. Thomas Lukasiewicz.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Peking University in 2018. During my Ph.D. period, I was co-advised by Prof. Zhifang Sui, Baobao Chang and Sujian Li.
